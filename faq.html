Frequently Asked Questions
How do I find a product that I want to purchase?
Enter your zip code on the homepage.
Type in the general name of your desired product and click submit.
Sort the list by the product name, price, store location, zip code, or the country in which the product is made.
Why should I enter products on this site?
This site is designed to benefit your economy.
This site encourages customers to purchase products that are made in their own country.
Purchasing products made in your own country will encourage companies to keep manufacturing jobs within your country.
How do I enter a product?
Enter your zip code (does not have to be the zip code of the store) to enter the site.
Go to the bottom of the page; fill in the fields; upload a picture; and click submit.
Make sure the zip code matches the address of the store.
