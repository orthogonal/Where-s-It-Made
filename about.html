What is "Where's It Made"?
This is a consumer-compiled database of products. Consumers enter information about any product including:
1. Item
2. Price
3. Description of item
4. Store Location of purchased product
5. Zip Code of store
6. Made in...
Why does this site exist?
1. Consumers should desire to benefit their own economy in their own country. When preparing to make a purchase, a customer can search this site in order to find a product made in their own country.
2. Purchasing items made in your own country encourages companies to keep jobs within the country instead of outsourcing jobs for cheaper labor. This, in turn, benefits the economy of your country.
